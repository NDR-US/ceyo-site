Evidentiary records for AI decision events.

CEYO produces deterministic, cryptographically sealed artifacts that allow AI-assisted decision events to be independently verified later without requiring default disclosure of model internals.

Record · Seal · Verify

Record

Policy-scoped capture defines what information may be recorded during an AI decision event.

Seal

Deterministic canonicalization and cryptographic hashing create tamper-evident artifacts.

Verify

Independent parties recompute canonicalization and validate signatures to confirm artifact integrity.

Purpose

Designed to be legible to executive, legal, technical, and security reviewers while remaining technically precise for independent verification.

Five steps

1 · Attach

Deploy as a boundary layer near inference execution.

2 · Capture

Policy-scoped decision data is recorded.

3 · Canonicalize

Data is normalized using deterministic canonicalization.

4 · Seal

Hashing and digital signatures create tamper-evident records.

5 · Verify

Third parties independently validate artifact integrity.
